How it works

Post a shift in 60 seconds.
Have workers secured in minutes.

No interviews. No agency middlemen. No paperwork shuffle. Just shifts filled by vetted workers, with all the compliance handled in the background.

1

Post your shift

Position, start/finish time, location. Compliant pay rates are calculated automatically from the Modern Award. Add instructions — attire, who to ask for, gate codes.

2

Workers secure it

Your shift goes to workers who match your requirements, location, and availability. Watch profiles roll in — no interviews needed. Review skills, ratings, and past work.

3

We handle the rest

Time tracking, award interpretation, payroll, super, workers comp. We're the Employer of Record. You get a single invoice — we pay everyone correctly.
