PART 03 — FOUNDATIONS

Voice.

Plain-spoken Aussie. Confident. Evidence-led.
Talk like a competent ops manager who respects the worker on the floor.

What we sound like

Concrete > clever.
Numbers > adjectives.

✓ Do

"Post a shift in under 60 seconds."
"We're the Employer of Record — we take the legal weight, you get the workers."
"Get paid the day after your shift."
"Top workers get first dibs on shifts."

✕ Don't

"Streamline your workforce procurement journey."
"We provide an EOR layer."
"Optimised payout cadences."
"Premium-tier operatives receive prioritised allocation."

One brand, three surfaces

Same system. Different tone
for each audience.

01 — Marketing site
Confident, evidence-led.
Big stamped CTAs. Real-photo hero cards. Numbers up top. The audience is making a decision — give them proof.
02 — Business platform
Operational, calm.
Dense tables, fixed sidebar, the compliance strip as the one brand moment. They're at work, not browsing.
03 — Worker app
Friendly, motivating.
Earnings on the hero. Streak stamps. Big tap-targets. Profile photos when we have them. Repeat engagement is the goal.
