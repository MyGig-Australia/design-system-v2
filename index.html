Brand

Identity

The wordmark sets the system: a hand-painted "my" next to a bold geometric "Gig". Human grit meets confident systems.

Wordmark — light background
myGig

Use on light backgrounds. Clear space = cap-height of "G" on all sides. Never distort or recolour.

Wordmark — dark background
myGig

White version on dark backgrounds. The "my" uses hi-vis on dark.

Tagline — hero & campaign use only
Work your Way

Always lowercase "your". Font: Permanent Marker. Hero shots and campaign layouts only — never in buttons, navigation, or product chrome.

Brand

Voice & Tone

Plain-spoken, confident, Aussie-flavoured. Competent operations manager who respects the worker on the floor. Not HR-speak; not Silicon Valley breezy.

SurfaceToneWhy
Marketing — BusinessConfident, evidence-led. Numbers, compliance, time saved.Decision makers want proof.
Marketing — WorkerEmpowering, freedom-first. "Work Your Way", flexibility, pay-on-time.Workers want agency.
Business appOperational, calm. Short labels, clear states, no jargon.They're at work, not browsing.
Worker appFriendly, motivating. Earnings highlighted, achievements celebrated.Repeat engagement matters.
Knowledge basePlain English regulatory. Reads like a Fair Work explainer.Stakes are real.

Guidelines

States & Motion

StateTreatment
HoverBackground shifts one step on scale. Stamp CTAs lift translate(-1px, -1px).
Press / activetranslate(2px, 2px) on stamp — shadow disappears. Feels like pressing a physical stamp.
Focus (keyboard)outline: 2px solid var(--hivis-500); outline-offset: 2px — never blue.
Disabledopacity: 0.45 only. No grey fill. Don't pretend it's clickable.
LoadingPulsing shimmer on 1.2s loop, ink-50 → ink-100. No spinners except pull-to-refresh.

Guidelines

Imagery

Real workers. Real worksites. Documentary-feeling. Never generate fake AI worker photography.

Photography guidelines
✓ Do
Warm-toned, natural light.
Hi-vis vests, aprons, hard hats, forklifts, hospo benches.
People mid-action — clocking in, stacking pallets, prepping food.
Faces visible. Trust is the product.
Light grain is acceptable.
✗ Don't
Posed studio shots or generic stock imagery.
Cold blue tech imagery or gradient overlays.
Generate fake AI worker photography.
Full-bleed photos behind text without a solid block.
Heavy filters or artificial lighting.
Photo: worker on shift
600 × 400
If real photography isn't available, use a black placeholder with a hi-vis corner label: "Photo: worker on shift". This signals intent without leaving empty space.
