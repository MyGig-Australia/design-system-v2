How it works

Four steps from problem to sorted.

01

Post your shift

Tell us the role, date, time, and location. Takes under 60 seconds. No phone calls, no account managers.

02

Workers accept

Pre-vetted workers in your area get notified instantly. They accept, and you get a confirmed crew list with profiles.

03

Shift runs

Workers clock in via the app. You see real-time attendance. Any issues? Amy is watching and will alert you immediately.

04

We pay everyone

Timesheets are auto-approved. We pay workers, handle super, tax, and compliance. You get one clean invoice.
